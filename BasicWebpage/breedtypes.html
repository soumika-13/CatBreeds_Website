Maine Coon

The Maine Coon is a large and muscular breed with a distinctive shaggy coat. They are known for their intelligence and playful personalities.

The British Shorthair is a sturdy and compact breed with a broad face and short, dense coat. They are known for their easy-going and affectionate nature.
