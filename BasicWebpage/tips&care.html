Maine Coon Tips & Care

  • Grooming: Brush your Maine Coon regularly to prevent matting and tangling.
  • Diet: Feed a high-quality, balanced diet to support their large size.
  • Exercise: Provide plenty of playtime and interactive toys to keep them active.
  • Health: Monitor for hip dysplasia and spinal muscular atrophy.

Persian Tips & Care

  • Grooming: Brush your Persian regularly to prevent hairballs and matting.
  • Diet: Feed a high-protein diet to support their energetic lifestyle.
  • Exercise: Provide plenty of playtime and interactive toys to keep them stimulated.
  • Health: Monitor for amyloidosis and respiratory issues.

British Short Hair Tips & Care

  • Grooming: Brush your British Shorthair weekly to prevent matting and tangling.
  • Diet: Feed a high-quality, balanced diet to maintain their sturdy build.
  • Exercise: Provide plenty of playtime and interactive toys to keep them active.
  • Health: Monitor for hypertrophic cardiomyopathy and patellar luxation.
